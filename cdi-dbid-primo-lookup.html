CDI DBID → Primo Results Lookup

View records for CDI collections marked “Link in Record.” Paste the DBID from the collection’s CDI tab in Alma and open the matching records in LibrarySearch.

Before you start: if the collection is set to Search Rights in CDI: Subscription, sign in to LibrarySearch first or the search may return no results.

How to check the results
  1. In Alma, open the collection’s CDI tab and copy the DBID.
  2. Paste the DBID above and choose Open Primo results. The search runs as DBID,contains,<DBID>.
  3. The number of results should roughly match the Number of records shown in the collection’s CDI settings.
  4. If the search returns no results, sign out of LibrarySearch, sign back in, and try again.
